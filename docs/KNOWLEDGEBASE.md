## 0. CHATBOT OPERATING INSTRUCTIONS (READ FIRST — APPLY TO EVERY RESPONSE)

These rules govern how the AI assistant must use this document. They are **non-negotiable**.

### 0.1 Grounding rule (anti-hallucination)
- **Only answer using information present in this document.** If a customer asks something that is not covered here, do **not** invent or infer an answer. Instead, respond with the fallback in §0.6.
- Do not extrapolate from one product to another (e.g., do not assume Personal Loan terms apply to Business Loans).
- Do not quote rates, fees, or limits unless they appear verbatim in this document. If a rate or fee is described as "competitive" or "market-linked" without a specific number, say so — do not guess.
- Do not mention competitor banks or compare I&M Bank to other banks.

### 0.2 Always recommend verification for time-sensitive items
For interest rates, fees, exchange rates, branch operating hours, eligibility decisions, and any approval outcome — always remind the customer that final figures are confirmed by the branch or relationship manager. Phrasing: *"For the latest rates and final eligibility, please confirm with the branch or call +254 20 322 1000."*

### 0.3 Never give financial, legal, or tax advice
The bot may explain product features, eligibility criteria, and processes documented here. The bot must **not**:
- Recommend which loan, account, card, or investment a customer should choose
- Forecast investment returns or interpret market movements
- Provide legal interpretations of terms and conditions
- Comment on a customer's specific tax, KRA, CRB, or estate-planning situation

For these topics, route the customer to a Relationship Manager or branch.

### 0.4 Never request or accept sensitive information
The bot must not ask for, accept, or repeat back: passwords, PINs, OTPs, full card numbers, CVVs, ID numbers, KRA PINs, or full account numbers. If a customer volunteers these, instruct them not to share them and to never share them with anyone — including someone claiming to be from I&M Bank.

### 0.5 Fraud and security protocol
If a customer reports a lost/stolen card, suspected fraud, unauthorized transaction, or phishing: immediately direct them to call **+254 719 088 000 / +254 20 322 1000 / 0800 721 088 (toll-free)** or visit the nearest branch. Do not attempt to triage further — escalate.

### 0.6 Fallback for out-of-scope questions
When a question is not covered by this document, respond:
> *"I don't have specific information on that in my reference material. For an accurate answer, please call I&M Bank Customer Care on +254 20 322 1000 or +254 719 088 000, email customercare@imbank.co.ke, or visit your nearest branch. I can also connect you with a live agent if you'd like."*

### 0.7 Tone
Professional, concise, courteous. Use customer's preferred language (English/Swahili) where possible. Avoid jargon — explain terms when they appear.

## 1. ABOUT I&M BANK KENYA

**Source:** https://www.imbankgroup.com/ke/about-us/who-we-are/

### 1.1 Overview
I&M Bank Limited is a leading commercial bank in Kenya, founded in **1974** as a financial services company (originally Investments & Mortgages Limited, based in Phoenix House, Nairobi) and formally converted to a commercial bank on **1 April 1996** following Central Bank of Kenya regulatory changes.

### 1.2 Group structure
I&M Bank is a wholly-owned subsidiary of **I&M Group PLC** (formerly I&M Holdings PLC), a public company listed on the **Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE)**.

I&M Group operates in **five countries** — Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Uganda, and Mauritius — through subsidiaries, affiliates, and joint ventures:
- **I&M Bank LIMITED** (Kenya) — the largest entity by asset value (~80% of group assets)
- **I&M Bank (T) Limited** (Tanzania)
- **I&M Bank (Rwanda) Plc**
- **I&M Burbidge Capital (U) Limited** (Uganda) — corporate finance advisory
- **Bank One Limited** (Mauritius) — 50% jointly held with CIEL Finance

Related Group companies include **GA Insurance Company** (one of Kenya's oldest general insurance companies, originally General Accident UK, incorporated 1979) and **I&M Bancassurance Intermediary Limited (I&MIL)** — the bank's bancassurance arm.

### 1.3 Mission, vision, values
- **Mission:** "To be partners of growth for all our stakeholders."
- **Vision:** "To become a company where the best people want to work, the first choice where customers want to do business, and where shareholders are happy with their investment."
- **Values:** Customer focus, employee development, shareholder value, fairness to partners.

### 1.4 Regulatory status
- Licensed and regulated by the **Central Bank of Kenya (CBK)**
- Listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE)
- Custody business licensed by the **Capital Markets Authority (CMA)** and **Central Depository & Settlements Corporation (CDSC)**

## 8. KEY FAQs

**Primary FAQ source:** https://www.imbankgroup.com/ke/faqs/

### 8.1 Loans

**Q: How do I apply for a loan?**
A: Log in to the I&M Bank OTG mobile app or web service. Opt in to the loan service by accepting the Terms & Conditions (first-time access). Then proceed to apply. If you face difficulties, visit your nearest branch.

**Q: What's the minimum and maximum unsecured personal loan via OTG?**
A: Min KES 50,000, max KES 3,000,000 — depending on eligibility and the limit assigned. Check your limit on the OTG app or web in the loan section.

**Q: Why has my loan limit changed?**
A: The limit is reviewed periodically based on account turnover, account activity, repayment history, CRB status, and product rules. It can increase or decrease.

**Q: How is my loan limit determined?**
A: Based on transaction history and loan repayment behaviour across all banks. Consistent on-time repayments contribute to limit growth and lower charges.

**Q: What are the unsecured personal loan charges?**
A: Processing fee 4% (one-off), excise duty 20% on the processing fee, credit life insurance premium 0.035%, and PHCF at 0.25% of the credit life premium. The interest rate varies per customer. Subject to change with market and regulatory conditions.

**Q: Can I apply for a Short-Term Loan if I have other loans?**
A: Yes — provided you remain eligible.

**Q: What is the Short-Term Loan amount range?**
A: KES 1,000 – KES 300,000.

**Q: What are the Short-Term Loan tenures?**
A: 1, 3, or 6 months. Monthly instalments apply only to the 3- and 6-month options.

**Q: Do I need a separate login for the Short-Term Loan?**
A: No. Use your existing I&M Bank OTG mobile app credentials.

**Q: Can I prepay my loan?**
A: Yes — tap "Repay Loan" on the I&M OTG App home screen, or via web services.

**Q: What if I miss a payment?**
A: You will incur additional fees and interest. Ensure your account is funded on the loan due date to avoid penalties.

**Q: Can I top up my loan?**
A: Yes — available to customers who have serviced their current loan instalments on time for **6 months** and meet top-up criteria. Apply via the OTG app.

**Q: Can I opt out of the loan service?**
A: Yes, you can opt out at any time **if you have no open loan**.

**Q: Do I need a KES account for the loan?**
A: Yes — a Kenyan Shilling account is required for disbursement, and the instalment is recovered from your operating account on the due date.

**Q: I dispute a CRB report — what should I do?**
A: Contact the CRB that issued the report. The bureau will reach out to I&M Bank to rectify any erroneous or inaccurate information.

### 8.2 Mortgages — Miliki Nyumba (Affordable Housing)

**Q: Can I use my existing plot toward the 10% contribution?**
A: Yes — that plot can form part of your 10% contribution. However, if there was a loan on the plot, the loans cannot be combined.

**Q: Can I take more than one Miliki Nyumba loan?**
A: No. Only one loan, and you must indicate it's a first-time homeowner application.

**Q: Can the Affordable Housing Loan be combined with other loans?**
A: No — it must be taken and paid independently of other loans.

**Q: What's the interest rate on Miliki Nyumba?**
A: Fixed at **9.5%** for the duration of the mortgage facility, up to 20 years (subject to retirement age).

**Q: What kind of property can I buy under Miliki Nyumba?**
A: A house from the **Boma Yangu** website, any developed home costing KES 10.5M or below, or self-construction. Residential houses only.

**Q: How long do I have to repay a mortgage?**
A: Up to 20 years for KES mortgages and up to 10 years for USD/EUR mortgages. The Weighted Average Life of mortgages is 8–9 years, so most loans are cleared earlier.

### 8.3 Cards & Milele Rewards

**Q: How do I enrol in the Milele Rewards Programme?**
A: Enrolment is **automatic** when you receive and activate your I&M Bank Debit or Credit Card. No separate sign-up.

**Q: Which transactions earn Milele Points?**
A: POS purchases and online (e-commerce) purchases on your I&M Debit or Credit Card. Recurring payments (utility bills, subscriptions) also qualify.

**Q: Which transactions don't earn Milele Points?**
A: ATM withdrawals, cash advances, fees and charges (interest, penalties, annual card fees), and refunded/reversed transactions.

**Q: What's the Milele Points value?**
A: 1 Milele Point = KES 1.

**Q: Are international transactions eligible?**
A: Yes — and you may earn triple points on international purchases.

**Q: Will I lose my Milele Points if my card is lost or stolen?**
A: No — points remain in your account. Report the lost/stolen card immediately for security; once a replacement is issued, points are accessible again.

**Q: How do I check my voucher balance?**
A: Dial **`*483*18#`** — the balance appears at the top of the dialog box.

**Q: Where can I redeem Milele Points?**
A: Travel (950+ airlines, 700+ hotels), cashback to your I&M account, eVouchers at 3,000+ local merchants (Carrefour, Naivas, Textbook Centre, Optica, Art Caffe, LC Waikiki, etc.).

**Q: When do points credit to my account?**
A: Typically the next business day after a POS or e-commerce transaction is processed. Some types may take longer.

### 8.4 Buy Now Pay Later

**Q: How do I apply for the Buy Now Pay Later loan?**
A: Apply via the I&M OTG mobile app or web services. Available to existing individual I&M Bank customers who meet eligibility criteria.

**Q: How does payment work at a participating outlet?**
A: I&M Bank pays the merchant on your behalf at the participating outlet, and you repay the bank over a flexible predetermined period.

### 8.5 Account Opening

**Q: What documents do I need to open a Sapphire Foreign Currency Account?**
A:
- **Kenyan Citizen:** National ID or Passport. Photo taken at the branch (or digital copy).
- **Foreign National:** Passport, Work Permit / Dependent Pass. Photo taken at the branch (or digital copy).

**Q: What's required for a Prepaid Card?**
A: Application form, original and copy of National ID or Passport, copy of KRA PIN, and a recent passport photo. Indicate the purpose, intended use, and frequency.

**Q: Can non-account holders use I&M services?**
A: Yes for several services — Bancassurance and Insurance Premium Financing are open to non-account holders. I&M FX Direct does not require an I&M Bank account either.

### 8.6 Digital Channels

**Q: How do I register for I&M On The Go (OTG)?**
A: You must be an I&M Bank Customer. Download the app from your App Store, or visit the web link. Register as a new user using your registered email; if you used a previous I&M mobile banking system, choose "forgot password" instead.

**Q: How do I authenticate transactions on OTG?**
A: Through SMS, email, or Google Authenticator.

**Q: Does OTG support joint approvers?**
A: Yes — for joint account holders, two joint approvers can authorise transactions.

**Q: Does I&M offer cash compensation for failed transfers?**
A: Yes — under the OTG Service Guarantee, if I&M fails to execute an Outgoing International (TT) or RTGS Local Transfer through the OTG Mobile/Web App due to an error on the bank's part, the bank proactively compensates the customer (T&Cs apply).

**Q: Is sending to M-PESA free?**
A: Yes — sending money from an I&M Bank personal account to an M-PESA number is free of charge.

### 8.7 Travel Insurance

**Q: How long is travel insurance coverage per trip?**
A: Up to **92 consecutive days per trip**, even with an annual policy.

**Q: Can non-residents get travel cover for visits to Kenya?**
A: Yes — via the **Inbound** travel insurance product.

### 8.8 Investments

**Q: What's the Employee Investment Loan Scheme (EILS)?**
A: A scheme that lets employees of partnering organisations borrow from I&M Bank to invest in treasury bonds. The loan is repaid via the employee's I&M salary account, with the treasury bond held as security.
