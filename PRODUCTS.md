# I&M Bank Kenya — Product Summary

## PERSONAL BANKING

### 💰 Accounts
- **Club Account** — flat-fee transaction account
- **PayGo Account** — pay-as-you-go transaction account
- **I&M Savers Account** — flexible savings
- **Children's Savings Account** — savings for kids
- **Sapphire FCY Account** — foreign currency account (8 currencies)
- **Foreign Currency Account** — USD, GBP, EUR, and regional currencies

### 💳 Cards

**Credit Cards**
- KES 0 joining fee on featured products
- Up to 50 days interest-free
- Premium tiers include lounge access, travel insurance
- Earn Milele Points on POS and online spend

**Debit Cards**
- KES 0 annual fees on featured products
- Unlimited airport lounge access
- Earn cash rewards
- 1M+ ATMs in 210+ countries

**Prepaid Cards**
- **Multicurrency Card** — USD 10 issuance, USD/GBP/EUR
- **Travel Card (USD)** — USD 8 issuance
- **Safari Card** — KES, no minimum load

### 🏦 Loans

**Unsecured Personal Loans**
- Via branch: up to KES 300,000 (1–60 months)
- Via OTG app: KES 50,000–3,000,000 (6–36 months)
- Processing: 4% fee, 20% excise duty, 0.035% credit life insurance

**Short-Term Loans**
- KES 1,000–300,000
- Tenures: 1, 3, or 6 months
- Instant disbursement via OTG app

**I&M@Work Loans**
- Up to KES 10 million
- Up to 96 months
- Via employer scheme

**Mortgages**
- Up to 20 years (KES); 10 years (USD/EUR)
- Residential or commercial property
- Deal Facilitation for diaspora customers

**Miliki Nyumba (Affordable Housing)**
- Homes up to KES 10.5M
- Fixed 9.5% interest rate
- 10% customer contribution
- Up to 20 years

**Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL)**
- For existing I&M customers
- Flexible repayment at participating merchants
- Apply via OTG app

### 🛡️ Insurance (Bancassurance)

**Motor Insurance** — own damage, theft, fire, third-party liability

**Travel Insurance**
- Outbound (Kenyans abroad)
- Inbound (visitors to Kenya)
- Annual or single-trip coverage
- Up to 92 days per trip

**Health Insurance** — inpatient, outpatient, no co-payment

**Other:** Education Plan, Golfer's Insurance

**Insurance Premium Financing (IPF)** — pay premiums in 10 instalments

### 💎 Investment & Custody Services
- Central Depository Services (CDS)
- Government securities trading (T-bills, T-bonds)
- Corporate bonds & commercial paper
- Employee Investment Loan Scheme (EILS)
- Offshore investments via Bank One (Mauritius)

### ⭐ Loyalty & Rewards
**I&M Milele Rewards** — Automatic enrolment with card activation
- Up to 2% points on POS and online purchases
- 1 Milele Point = KES 1
- Redeem for: Travel (950+ airlines, 700+ hotels), cashback, eVouchers (3,000+ merchants)
- Voucher balance: dial `*483*18#`

### 🌟 Premium Services

**Select Banking**
- Dedicated Relationship Manager
- Eligibility by invitation or KES 2,000/month subscription
- Pre-approved KES 1,500,000 loan facility
- Select Centres (Sarit, Hannover/Riverside)
- Priority queueing, lounge access

**Premium Banking**
- Special Club Account
- Lifestyle cards
- Wealth Management Solutions
- Offshore banking
