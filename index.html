1. About I&M Bank Kenya

Knowledgebase

1.1 Overview

I&M Bank Limited is a leading commercial bank in Kenya, founded in 1974 as a financial services company (originally Investments & Mortgages Limited, based in Phoenix House, Nairobi) and formally converted to a commercial bank on 1 April 1996 following Central Bank of Kenya regulatory changes.

1.2 Group Structure

I&M Bank is a wholly-owned subsidiary of I&M Group PLC (formerly I&M Holdings PLC), a public company listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE).

I&M Group operates in five countries — Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Uganda, and Mauritius — through subsidiaries, affiliates, and joint ventures:

  • I&M Bank LIMITED (Kenya) — the largest entity by asset value (~80% of group assets)
  • I&M Bank (T) Limited (Tanzania)
  • I&M Bank (Rwanda) Plc
  • I&M Burbidge Capital (U) Limited (Uganda) — corporate finance advisory
  • Bank One Limited (Mauritius) — 50% jointly held with CIEL Finance

Related Group companies include GA Insurance Company (one of Kenya's oldest general insurance companies, originally General Accident UK, incorporated 1979) and I&M Bancassurance Intermediary Limited (I&MIL) — the bank's bancassurance arm.

1.3 Mission, Vision & Values

  • Mission: "To be partners of growth for all our stakeholders."
  • Vision: "To become a company where the best people want to work, the first choice where customers want to do business, and where shareholders are happy with their investment."
  • Values: Customer focus, employee development, shareholder value, fairness to partners.

1.4 Regulatory Status

  • Licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of Kenya (CBK)
  • Listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE)
  • Custody business licensed by the Capital Markets Authority (CMA) and Central Depository & Settlements Corporation (CDSC)
  • Pension custody registered with the Retirement Benefits Authority (RBA)

Knowledgebase

3.1 Personal Transaction Accounts

Two transaction account options:

  • Club Account — flat-fee account
  • PayGo Account — pay-as-you-go account

Both accounts include access to electronic banking, International Visa Cards, and IMBA loyalty points. A Foreign Currency Transaction Account is also available in 8 major currencies.

3.2 Personal Savings Accounts

  • I&M Savers Account — for general savings goals
  • Children's Savings Account — designed to build savings habits for children
  • Foreign Currency Savings options — available in Kenya Shillings and major foreign currencies

3.3 Foreign Currency Accounts

The Sapphire Foreign Currency Account and Foreign Currency (FCY) Account are offered in 8 major currencies: USD, GBP, EUR, AUD, ZAR, JPY, CHF, and others.

Account Opening — Sapphire FCY (Individuals)

  • Kenyan Citizens: National ID card or Passport. Photo taken at branch (or digital copy).
  • Foreign Nationals: Passport, Work Permit / Dependent Pass. Photo taken at branch (or digital copy).

3.5 Personal Loans

3.5.1 Unsecured Personal Loan (Branch)

  • Loan amount: Up to KES 300,000
  • Tenure: 1 to 60 months
  • Unsecured (no collateral required)
  • Quick turnaround — applications processed within 24 hours
  • Credit life insurance included

3.5.2 Unsecured Personal Loan — via OTG (Digital)

  • Loan amount: Min KES 50,000 to max KES 3,000,000
  • Tenure: 6 to 36 months
  • Processing fee: 4% of loan amount (one-off)
  • Excise duty: 20% charged on the processing fee
  • Credit life insurance premium: 0.035% (with PHCF at 0.25% of the credit life premium)
  • Top-up loans available after 6 months of timely repayments
  • A Kenyan Shilling account is required for disbursement

3.5.3 I&M@Work Unsecured Personal Loan

  • Unsecured personal loan up to KES 10 million
  • Repayment tenure up to 96 months
  • Available via employers under the I&M@Work scheme

3.5.4 I&M Short-Term Loan

  • Quick unsecured personal loan, disbursed instantly
  • Loan amount: KES 1,000 – KES 300,000
  • Tenure options: 1 month, 3 months, or 6 months
  • Apply via the I&M Bank OTG Mobile App

3.5.5 Home Loans / Mortgages

  • Tenure: Up to 20 years (KES); up to 10 years (USD/EUR)
  • Types: Residential, Commercial, Foreign Currency (USD-denominated)
  • Deal Facilitation services offered via the Diaspora Banking Unit (free for diaspora customers signing up for I&M mortgage services)
  • Weighted Average Life (WAL) of mortgages is typically 8–9 years

3.5.6 Miliki Nyumba (Affordable Housing Mortgage)

  • Aligned to Kenya's affordable housing scheme via the Boma Yangu programme
  • Eligible properties: any developed home priced KES 10.5M and below, or self-construction
  • For residential houses only
  • Customer contribution: 10% (an existing plot can count toward the 10%)
  • Only one Miliki Nyumba loan per first-time homeowner
  • Interest rate: Fixed at 9.5% for the duration, up to 20 years

3.5.7 Buy Now Pay Later (BNPL)

  • Unsecured loan for existing individual I&M Bank customers
  • I&M Bank pays the merchant directly on the customer's behalf
  • Apply via the I&M Bank OTG mobile app or web services

Travel Insurance

  • Outbound (Kenyan residents travelling abroad) and Inbound (non-Kenyan residents coming to Kenya)

Health (Medical) Insurance

  • Pre-existing and chronic conditions covered up to an agreed limit

Insurance Premium Financing (IPF)

  • Open to account holders and non-account holders
  • Available for general insurance policies — excludes Life insurance
  • No collateral required

8. Key FAQs

Knowledgebase

8.1 Loans

How do I apply for a loan?
Log in to the I&M Bank OTG mobile app or web service. Opt in to the loan service by accepting the Terms & Conditions (first-time access). Then proceed to apply. If you face difficulties, visit your nearest branch.
What's the minimum and maximum unsecured personal loan via OTG?
Min KES 50,000, max KES 3,000,000 — depending on eligibility and the limit assigned. Check your limit on the OTG app or web in the loan section.
What are the unsecured personal loan charges?
Processing fee 4% (one-off), excise duty 20% on the processing fee, credit life insurance premium 0.035%, and PHCF at 0.25% of the credit life premium. The interest rate varies per customer.
Can I apply for a Short-Term Loan if I have other loans?
Yes — provided you remain eligible.
What are the Short-Term Loan amounts and tenures?
Amounts: KES 1,000 – KES 300,000. Tenures: 1, 3, or 6 months. Monthly instalments apply only to the 3- and 6-month options.
Can I prepay my loan?
Yes — tap "Repay Loan" on the I&M OTG App home screen, or via web services.
Can I top up my loan?
Yes — available to customers who have serviced their current loan instalments on time for 6 months and meet top-up criteria. Apply via the OTG app.
Do I need a KES account for the loan?
Yes — a Kenyan Shilling account is required for disbursement, and the instalment is recovered from your operating account on the due date.

8.2 Mortgages — Miliki Nyumba

What's the interest rate for Miliki Nyumba?
Fixed at 9.5% for the duration of the mortgage facility, up to 20 years (subject to retirement age).
Can I use my existing plot toward the 10% contribution?
Yes — that plot can form part of your 10% contribution. However, if there was a loan on the plot, the loans cannot be combined.
Can I take more than one Miliki Nyumba loan?
No. Only one loan, and you must indicate it's a first-time homeowner application.
How long do I have to repay a mortgage?
Up to 20 years for KES mortgages and up to 10 years for USD/EUR mortgages. The Weighted Average Life of mortgages is 8–9 years, so most loans are cleared earlier.

8.3 Cards & Milele Rewards

How do I enrol in the Milele Rewards Programme?
Enrolment is automatic when you receive and activate your I&M Bank Debit or Credit Card. No separate sign-up.
What's the value of Milele Points?
1 Milele Point = KES 1.
Will I lose my Milele Points if my card is lost or stolen?
No — points remain in your account. Report the lost/stolen card immediately for security; once a replacement is issued, points are accessible again.
How do I check my voucher balance?
Dial *483*18# — the balance appears at the top of the dialog box.

8.4 Digital Channels

How do I register for I&M On The Go (OTG)?
You must be an I&M Bank Customer. Download the app from your App Store, or visit the web link. Register as a new user using your registered email; if you used a previous I&M mobile banking system, choose "forgot password" instead.
Is sending to M-PESA free?
Yes — sending money from an I&M Bank personal account to an M-PESA number is free of charge.
Does I&M offer cash compensation for failed transfers?
Yes — under the OTG Service Guarantee, if I&M fails to execute an Outgoing International (TT) or RTGS Local Transfer through the OTG Mobile/Web App due to an error on the bank's part, the bank proactively compensates the customer (T&Cs apply).

Frequently Asked Questions

Reference

Loans & Borrowing

How do I apply for a loan?
Log in to I&M OTG mobile app or web service, accept Terms & Conditions, view your loan limit, and apply. Visit your branch if you need help.
What's the range for unsecured personal loans?
Min KES 50,000 to max KES 3,000,000 (depends on eligibility). Tenure: 6 to 36 months.
Why did my loan limit change?
Limits are reviewed periodically based on account activity, CRB status, repayment history, and product rules.
What are the charges on OTG personal loans?
Processing fee: 4% (one-off). Excise duty: 20% on the processing fee. Credit life insurance: 0.035%. PHCF: 0.25% of the credit life premium. Interest rate: varies per customer.
Can I apply for a Short-Term Loan if I have other loans?
Yes, if you remain eligible.
What are Short-Term Loan amounts and tenures?
Amounts: KES 1,000–300,000. Tenures: 1, 3, or 6 months (instalments for 3 & 6 months only).
Can I prepay my loan?
Yes — tap "Repay Loan" on OTG app or use web services.
What if I miss a payment?
You'll incur additional fees and interest. Ensure funds are available on the due date.
Can I top up my loan?
Yes — after 6 months of timely repayments, apply via OTG app if you meet top-up criteria.
Can I opt out of the loan service?
Yes, anytime when you have no open loan.
Do I need a KES account?
Yes — a Kenyan Shilling account is required for disbursement and instalment recovery.
What if I dispute a CRB report?
Contact the CRB that issued the report; they'll reach out to I&M to rectify errors.

Mortgages & Miliki Nyumba

What's the interest rate for Miliki Nyumba?
Fixed at 9.5% for the duration, up to 20 years (subject to retirement age).
Can I use my existing plot toward the 10% contribution?
Yes — an existing plot can count toward the 10% contribution, but loans on that plot cannot be combined.
Can I take more than one Miliki Nyumba loan?
No — only one loan per first-time homeowner.
Can Miliki Nyumba be combined with other loans?
No — it must be taken and paid independently.
What properties are eligible for Miliki Nyumba?
Homes from Boma Yangu website, any developed home up to KES 10.5M, or self-construction (residential only).
How long can I take to repay a mortgage?
Up to 20 years (KES); up to 10 years (USD/EUR). Average life: 8–9 years.

Cards & Milele Rewards

How do I enrol in Milele Rewards?
Automatic enrolment when you activate your I&M Debit or Credit Card — no separate sign-up.
Which transactions earn points?
POS purchases, online (e-commerce), and recurring payments (bills, subscriptions).
Which transactions don't earn points?
ATM withdrawals, cash advances, fees, interest, penalties, annual card fees, refunded/reversed transactions.
What's the value of Milele Points?
1 Milele Point = KES 1.
Do international purchases earn points?
Yes — and you may earn triple points on international transactions.
Will I lose points if my card is lost/stolen?
No — points remain in your account. Report immediately; points become accessible once replacement is issued.
How do I check my voucher balance?
Dial *483*18# — balance shown at top of dialog.
Where can I redeem Milele Points?
Travel (950+ airlines, 700+ hotels), cashback to I&M account, eVouchers at 3,000+ merchants (Carrefour, Naivas, Textbook Centre, Optica, Art Caffe, LC Waikiki, etc.).

Accounts & Cards

What documents do I need for a Foreign Currency Account?
Kenyan Citizen: National ID or Passport + photo. Foreign National: Passport + Work Permit/Dependent Pass + photo.
What's required for a Prepaid Card?
Application form, ID (original + copy), KRA PIN copy, passport photo. Indicate purpose, use, and frequency.
Can non-account holders use I&M services?
Yes — Bancassurance, Insurance Premium Financing, and I&M FX Direct don't require an I&M account.

Digital Channels

How do I register for I&M On The Go (OTG)?
Download from App Store/Play Store or visit web link. Register with your I&M-registered email. If upgrading from old system, use "forgot password".
How do I authenticate transactions on OTG?
Via SMS, email, or Google Authenticator.
Does OTG support joint approvers?
Yes — for joint account holders, two joint approvers can authorize transactions.
Does I&M offer compensation for failed transfers?
Yes — under OTG Service Guarantee, if I&M fails to execute an International (TT) or RTGS Local Transfer due to bank error, compensation applies (T&Cs).
Is sending to M-PESA free?
Yes — sending from your I&M Bank personal account to M-PESA is free.

Insurance

How long is travel insurance coverage per trip?
Up to 92 consecutive days per trip, even with annual policies.
Can non-residents get travel cover?
Yes — via Inbound travel insurance for visitors to Kenya.
What does health insurance cover?
Inpatient, outpatient, pre-existing conditions (up to agreed limit), maternity, dental, optical. No co-payment for inpatient/outpatient.
What does motor insurance cover?
Accidental own damage, theft, fire, and third-party liability.

Buy Now Pay Later

How do I apply for Buy Now Pay Later?
Apply via I&M OTG app or web services (existing I&M customers only).
How does payment work at outlets?
I&M Bank pays the merchant on your behalf; you repay the bank over a flexible predetermined period.

Investments & Custody

What's the Employee Investment Loan Scheme (EILS)?
A scheme letting employees borrow from I&M to invest in treasury bonds, with repayment via salary and the bond as security.
What services does Custody offer?
Central Depository Services (CDS), government securities, corporate bonds, offshore investments, nominee services, corporate actions.

General

Who regulates I&M Bank?
Central Bank of Kenya (CBK). Also licensed by CMA, CDSC, and RBA for custody/investment services.
What are I&M Bank's mission and values?
Mission: "Partners of growth for all stakeholders." Values: Customer focus, employee development, shareholder value, fairness.
When was I&M Bank founded?
Founded in 1974 as Investments & Mortgages Limited; converted to commercial bank 1 April 1996.
Is I&M Bank listed?
Yes — I&M Bank is a subsidiary of I&M Group PLC, listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE).

Chatbot Operating Rules

Reference

Version: 1.0 · Updated: 6 May 2026 · Applies to: AI Chatbot responses powered by I&M Bank Kenya Knowledgebase

Rule 1: Grounding (Anti-Hallucination)

✅ DO:
Answer only using information in the knowledgebase
Quote rates, fees, and limits verbatim from the document
Say "I don't have that information" when unsure
Use the fallback response for out-of-scope questions
❌ DON'T:
Invent or infer information not in the document
Extrapolate one product's terms to another
Guess at specific rates, fees, or limits
Compare I&M Bank to competitors
Provide information from external sources

Rule 2: Verification Reminder

⚠️ Always include for all time-sensitive items (rates, fees, exchange rates, branch hours, eligibility):
"For the latest rates and final eligibility, please confirm with the branch or call +254 20 322 1000."

Rule 3: Never Give Advice

  • ❌ Financial advice (e.g., "You should get this loan")
  • ❌ Legal interpretations of terms
  • ❌ Tax or KRA advice
  • ❌ Investment forecasts or market predictions
  • ❌ Recommendations on which product to choose

Instead, route to: Relationship Manager, Branch staff, Qualified professional (tax, legal), or Custody & Investment Services advisor.

Rule 4: Sensitive Information Protocol

❌ NEVER ask for or accept: Passwords or PINs · One-Time Passwords (OTPs) · Full card numbers or CVVs · ID numbers or full account numbers · KRA PINs

If a customer volunteers this info: instruct them "Do not share this with anyone, including I&M Bank staff." Recommend they report to security if suspicious. Escalate to fraud hotline if needed.

Rule 5: Fraud & Security Escalation

🚨 IMMEDIATE ESCALATION if customer reports: Lost or stolen card · Suspected fraud · Unauthorized transaction · Phishing or suspicious communication

Direct to: +254 719 088 000 (primary) · +254 20 322 1000 · 0800 721 088 (toll-free) · Nearest branch

Do NOT: Attempt to troubleshoot · Ask for account details · Delay the escalation

Rule 6: Out-of-Scope Response

"I don't have specific information on that in my reference material. For an accurate answer, please call I&M Bank Customer Care on +254 20 322 1000 or +254 719 088 000, email customercare@imbank.co.ke, or visit your nearest branch. I can also connect you with a live agent if you'd like."

Rule 7: Tone & Language

  • Professional — use business language
  • Concise — keep responses brief and clear

  • Avoid jargon — explain technical terms
